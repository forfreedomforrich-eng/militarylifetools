BAH Calculator Guide: How Basic Allowance for Housing Works

July 14, 2026 | 7 min read | By Military Life Tools

Basic Allowance for Housing, or BAH, is one of the most important monthly benefits for many active-duty service members. It helps offset housing costs when you are not living in government-provided quarters. Because a housing allowance can change based on rank, duty station, and dependency status, many people search for a BAH calculator before making a housing plan.

What affects BAH?

  • Rank or pay grade
  • Duty location
  • Whether you have dependents
  • Whether you are living in the barracks or off-base

Why this matters

A simple estimate helps you understand how much housing support you may receive and how that fits into your monthly budget. It does not replace official rates, but it is useful for planning before a move or relocation.
