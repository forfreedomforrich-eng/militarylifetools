Military Retirement Planning Guide: Legacy, BRS, and TSP

July 14, 2026 | 8 min read | By Military Life Tools

Military retirement planning can feel complicated because the rules are different depending on when you joined and which system applies to you. A simple explanation helps you understand whether the legacy pension, BRS, or TSP strategy makes more sense for your goals.

Key questions many members ask

  • Which retirement system applies to me?
  • How do Legacy and BRS differ?
  • How much does TSP contribution matter?
  • What does a retirement calculator estimate?

Why this matters

Retirement planning works best when you understand the basic structure first. The calculator gives you a starting point, but the bigger decision is how to combine pension, TSP, and lifestyle expectations over time.

Helpful tool

Use our retirement calculator to estimate pension value, compare Legacy vs BRS, and review how TSP factors into your planning.

Bottom line

The earlier you understand your retirement system, the easier it becomes to make decisions about serving longer, contributing to TSP, and planning for the transition to civilian life.
