## 3. Ready-to-publish article starter

### VA Disability article summary
VA disability compensation is one of the most important benefits available to veterans, but it is also one of the most misunderstood. Many veterans want to understand how ratings work, what compensation may look like, and whether they should file a claim or appeal an existing decision.

The best approach is to start with a clear explanation, use a calculator for planning, and gather strong medical evidence before making decisions.

### GI Bill article summary
The Post-9/11 GI Bill is one of the most valuable education benefits available to veterans and service members. Before choosing a school, many people want to estimate tuition coverage, monthly housing allowance, and books support to avoid surprises.

A GI Bill calculator can help turn a confusing benefit into a clearer estimate before enrolling.

### Retirement article summary
Military retirement planning is easier when service members understand the difference between Legacy and BRS, plus how TSP contributes to long-term income. A retirement calculator can help estimate pension value and make planning feel more actionable.
