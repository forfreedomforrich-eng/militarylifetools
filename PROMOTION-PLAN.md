# Promotion Plan for Military Life Tools

## Priority 1: Low-risk publishing channels
- WordPress / Ghost / Blogger / Medium
- X / Twitter
- Facebook Page / Facebook Group
- LinkedIn
- Telegram / Discord

## Priority 2: Content angles for posts
- BAH calculator guide
- TSP and retirement guide
- Military leave calculator guide
- PCS move checklist guide
- VA loan calculator guide

## Priority 3: Publishing rhythm
- Publish 2 short posts per week on social channels
- Publish 1 longer article or guide every 3-4 days to owned properties
- Rotate between tools and practical guides

## Priority 4: Post format template
- Hook: a real problem service members face
- Value: one practical takeaway
- Link: one relevant tool or guide
- CTA: ask readers to try the tool or compare with their own situation

## Priority 5: Avoidance rules
- Do not post spammy repetitive links
- Do not overuse affiliate language
- Keep the tone helpful and practical
- Use real military-life examples
